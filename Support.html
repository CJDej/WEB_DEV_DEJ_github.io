Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my game crashing on startup? +

This is usually caused by outdated graphics drivers or insufficient system requirements. Make sure your PC meets the minimum requirements and update your graphics drivers. You can also try verifying game files through the launcher.

How do I request a refund? +

Refunds can be requested within 14 days of purchase if you've played less than 2 hours. Go to your purchase history and select "Request Refund" on the game you want to return.

My account was hacked, what should I do? +

Immediately use our account recovery tool and enable two-factor authentication. Contact our security team at security@battlemania.com with details of the issue.

Why can't I connect to multiplayer matches? +

This could be due to firewall settings, NAT type restrictions, or server maintenance. Check our server status page first, then verify your network settings aren't blocking the game.
